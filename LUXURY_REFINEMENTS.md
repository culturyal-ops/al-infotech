# Luxury Refinements - Agency-Quality Polish

## Overview
These refinements elevate the site from "good template" to "boutique agency quality" by focusing on premium details, restraint, and sophisticated visual hierarchy.

---

## ✨ Implemented Refinements

### 1. Hero Text Readability Enhancement
**Problem**: Green heading text was getting lost against the background.

**Solution**:
- Darkened hero overlay gradient (0.82 → 0.88 on left side)
- Increased middle section opacity (0.35 → 0.45)
- Adjusted white text to `text-white/95` for subtle desaturation
- Better contrast without losing the cinematic feel

**Result**: Text now pops clearly while maintaining luxury aesthetic.

---

### 2. Premium Navbar Glass Effect
**Problem**: Navbar felt too opaque and heavy.

**Solution**:
- Increased blur: `blur(20-32px)` instead of `blur(16-24px)`
- Reduced opacity: `rgba(0,0,0,0.35-0.50)` instead of light background
- Subtle white border: `rgba(255,255,255,0.08-0.12)`
- Refined gradient overlay for depth
- Changed text to `text-white/90` with subtle shadow

**Result**: True glassmorphism effect - feels floating and premium.

---

### 3. CTA Button Proportion Refinement
**Problem**: "BOOK NOW" button was oversized and dominating the hero.

**Solution**:
- Reduced padding: `15px 36px` (was `18px 40px`)
- Maintains presence without overwhelming
- Better visual balance with nav items

**Result**: Button feels intentional, not aggressive.

---

### 4. Hero Content Width Constraint
**Problem**: Text block was too wide, reducing luxury feel.

**Solution**:
- Changed from `max-w-[820px]` to `max-w-2xl` (672px)
- Creates more breathing room
- Follows luxury design principle: "less is more"

**Result**: Content feels curated and intentional.

---

### 5. Subtle Hero Background Motion
**Problem**: Static background felt flat.

**Solution**:
- Added ultra-slow zoom: `scale(1.08 → 1)` over 20 seconds
- Creates subtle depth and movement
- Not distracting, just premium

**Result**: Adds life without being gimmicky.
