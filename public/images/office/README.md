# Office Images

Place office photos here.

## Suggested Photos

- `office-exterior.jpg` - Front view of the office building
- `office-interior.jpg` - Reception or main workspace
- `meeting-room.jpg` - Client consultation area
- `team-at-work.jpg` - Staff working/coordinating

## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Size**: Optimize to under 1MB per image
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 1200px width
- **Style**: Professional, well-lit, clean spaces

These images appear in the Team section on the About page.
